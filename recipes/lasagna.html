Orange Chicken

Home

Classic Chinese orange chicken.

This recipe is a tasty, high protein meal that can be cooked in under an hour!

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup Cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup Flour
  • 1 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into bite sizes.
  2. Dredge chicken in whisked eggs and cornstarch/flour mixture.
  3. Place orange juce, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and red chili flakes in small pot and cook over medium heat.
  4. Heat oil in a pan to 350 degrees. Once ready, cook chicken in small batches for 2 minutes each batch.
  5. Remove from oil and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  6. Toss fried chicken with the sweet orange sauce.
  7. Add to rice, or serve chicken and sauce by itself. Enjoy!
